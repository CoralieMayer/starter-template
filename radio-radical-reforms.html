Radio Radical Reform Seminar

Radio Radical Reform is a fictional seminar about bringing the medium of radio into the future. The design of the information booklet is based on 60s-70s audio device designs and incorporates digital monospace typefaces to give it a retro-modern feel. Winner of the 2021 GDC Salazar Award for Print Design.
